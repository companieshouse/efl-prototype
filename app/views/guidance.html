How to prepare the document

You must complete the document in advance and save it to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

Uploading a Companies House form

You can complete the form electronically:

  1. Find the Companies House form that you need and download it.
  2. Type the information directly onto the form.
  3. Save the form to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

You can complete the form using a pen:

  • Print the form.
  • Fill in the information directly onto the form using a pen.
  • Scan the completed form.
  • Save the scanned form to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

Signatures

Uploading other types of document

  1. Prepare the document in advance, such as a letter or report.
  2. Save the document to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

Document formats you can upload

Accepted document formats:

  1. PDF
  2. Word
  3. Excel

Documents that have a fee

You can use this service to pay for the document you are uploading if it has a fee.

You will need a credit or debit card.

The documents that have a fee are:

  1. Document (£fee)

How to pay:

  1. Upload the completed document.
  2. Select ‘pay for the document online’ which will open a new tab or window.
  3. Enter your payment details.
  4. You will be shown a payment reference on the confirmation screen when you have paid.
  5. Make a note of the reference and return to the service.
  6. Enter the reference into the payment reference box.
  7. Submit the document.

What happens next

You will receive a confirmation email with your reference number.

We will process the uploaded document.

If your document is acceptable for filing:

  1. you will receive an email to confirm the document was accepted.
  2. the document will be registered with the date it was uploaded to Companies House.

If your document is not acceptable for filing we will email you to let you know what to do next.

Contact us

Email us at enquiries@companieshouse.gov.uk and we'll answer as soon as possible.

Our telephone contact centre is now closed to protect the welfare of our employees during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak.
