How to prepare the document

You must complete the document in advance and save it to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

Companies House form

  1. Find the form you need and download it.
  2. Complete the form.

You can do this:

Electronically:

  • Type the information directly onto the form.
  • Save the form to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

    • Using a pen:

    • Print the form.
    • Fill in the information directly onto the form using a pen.
    • Scan the completed form.
    • Save the scanned form to the device you are using so that it is ready to upload.

      Documents that have a fee

      You can use this service to pay for the document if it has a fee.

      You will need a credit or debit card.

      The documents that have a fee are:

      1. Document (£fee)

      How to pay:

      1. Upload the completed document.
      2. Select ‘pay for the document online’ which will open a new tab.
      3. Enter your payment details.
      4. You will be shown a payment reference on the confirmation screen when you have paid.
      5. Make a note of the reference and return to the service.
      6. Enter the reference into the payment reference box.
      7. Submit the document.

      What happens next

      You will receive a confirmation email with your reference number.

      We will process the uploaded document.

      If the document is acceptable for filing:

      1. you will receive an email to confirm the document was accepted.
      2. the document will be registered with the date it was uploaded to Companies House.

      If the document is not acceptable for filing:

      1. we will email you to let you know what to do next.
