Details

You must pay the £17 fee online before you upload and submit this document.

How to submit this document online

1. Download and complete the document

Save the document to the device you're using after you have completed it.

2. Pay for the document

Pay the £17 fee for the SQP CS01 online with a credit or debit card using GOV.UK Pay (opens in a new tab or window).

3. Make a note of your payment reference number

This will be shown on the confirmation screen and confirmation email after you have paid the fee. You will need this to upload your document.

4. Upload the document

Use the Upload a document to Companies House service to send us your document online.
