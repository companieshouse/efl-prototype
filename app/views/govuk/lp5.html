Apply for a registration of a limited partnership (LP5)

Use this form as an application for registration of a limited partnership. It costs £20 to file this form.

From: Companies House

Documents

LP5 Application for registration of a limited partnership

PDF, 35.1KB, 3 pages

Details

You must pay the £20 fee online before you upload and submit this document.

You can continue to use form LP5 to register a limited partnership in Scotland until 24 July 2017

To apply for registration of a limited partnership which is to be designated as a private fund limited partnership, use form LP7

How to submit this document online during the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak

1. Download and complete the document

Save the document to the device you're using after you have completed it.

2. Pay for the document

Pay the £20 fee for your LP5 application online using GOV.UK Pay (opens in a new tab or window).

3. Make a note of your payment reference number

This will be shown on the confirmation screen and confirmation email after you have paid the fee. You will need this to upload your document.

4. Upload the document

Use the Upload a document to Companies House service to send us your document online.
