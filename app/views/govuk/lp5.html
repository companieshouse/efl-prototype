Details

You can continue to use form LP5 to register a limited partnership in Scotland until 24 July 2017

It costs £20 to file this form. You must pay the fee online before you upload the form.

To apply for registration of a limited partnership which is to be designated as a private fund limited partnership, use form LP7

How to pay the fee

You must pay the £20 fee using GOV.UK Pay before uploading your LP5 application.

Pay the fee for your LP5 application

Once the payment is complete, you’ll get a payment reference number.
