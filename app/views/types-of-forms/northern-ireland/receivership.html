How to upload 3.04, 3.05 and 3.12 documents

You must merge these forms first and upload them as one document.

To upload form 3.04 with form 3.05:
Form 3.05 should be at the front, followed by form 3.04.

To upload form 3.12 with form 3.05:
Form 3.12 should be at the front, followed by form 3.05.

To upload form 3.12 with form 3.05 and form 3.04:
Form 3.12 should be at the front, followed by form 3.05, then form 3.04.

Help with merging documents

To merge documents, you could:

  • use an online tool or app that merges PDF documents
  • create a new document and combine saved files into a single PDF
  • scan documents together if you have access to a printer or photocopier, and save the scan as a single PDF
